# Retail-Stores-USA-Power-BI-Dashboard

Overview
An end-to-end Power BI dashboard analyzing 9,994 retail transactions ($2.3M revenue) across US stores from 2015–2018. Built as 5 interconnected pages to support both executive-level KPI tracking and drill-down analysis for regional and category performance.
Key metrics tracked: Total Revenue ($2.29M), Profit Margin (12.5%), Total Orders (5,009), Avg Days to Ship (4.0), Total Quantity (37.9K units)
Dashboard Pages

Performance Dashboard — company-wide KPIs, revenue/profit trend (2015–2018), sales by category/region/state
Geographic Analysis — state-level drill-through map, shipping time by state, region sales vs target
Profitability Analysis — profit waterfall by sub-category (identifies where the business wins and loses margin), profit margin by state
Sales Deep Dive — monthly sales heatmap for seasonality detection, 80/20 Pareto analysis of sub-categories, profit margin by sub-category
Segment & Customer Analysis — sales trend by customer segment, shipping mode distribution, segment × category revenue matrix

Key Insights
Technology drives 50.8% of total sales, the single largest category
Tables is the only sub-category losing money (-$18K profit), identified via waterfall analysis
West region leads in sales ($725K) but is furthest from its $798K target
59.8% of orders ship via Standard Class; Consumer segment contributes the most revenue and profit across every category
Phones and Chairs are the top 2 sub-categories by sales volume, together driving a large share of revenue

Tools Used
Power BI (DAX measures, drill-through, bookmarks/navigation, waterfall & Pareto visuals)
